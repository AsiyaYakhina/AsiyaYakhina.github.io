Brainstorm Facilitation

Using design thinking principles to lead creative ideation meetings

During one of my semesters at Mobile Experience Lab at MIT, I organized brainstorming sessions for the lab members, as well as for outside clients such as General Sensing and Jaguar. I worked on creating an environment that is conducive to a productive ideation process by helping the participants engage in discussion, and get inspired by their surroundings and each other. I also designed complementary materials that informed the ideation process and helped bring all participants to the same page.

One type of material that we designed individually for various clients, is a value map. The purpose of this template is to help participants focus on the customer during the ideation process and document their ideas in a comprehensive readable form:

After the participants are done brainstorming, they can use this presentation template to document a particular idea into a comprehensive report to be used during the presentation:
