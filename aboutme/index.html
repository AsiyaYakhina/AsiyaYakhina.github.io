I have graduated with Bachelors in Media Arts and Sciences from Wellesley College in Spring of 2016. My degree combines classes in computer science, design, and art. This training has enabled me to participate in all stages of design process, complete multiple projects from start to finish, and collaborate effectively in interdisciplinary teams.

Over the last three years I have completed a UX internship at Microsoft, worked as a user interface and experience designer at two startups, and led user research at the Mobile Experience Lab at MIT. These experiences taught me how to prototype user interfaces, how to test the prototypes with users, and how to make data-informed design decisions.

In my free time I enjoy playing and making video games, and cooking.
